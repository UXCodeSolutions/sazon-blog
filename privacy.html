Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Sazón Blog is an independent digital portal dedicated to news and trends in the culinary world. We take our readers' privacy very seriously.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information and only when voluntarily provided:

  • Subscription email: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we will store your email address solely for sending the newsletter.
  • Contact information: If you write to us through the contact form, we will store your name, email and message in order to respond.
  • Browsing data: We collect anonymous site usage data through analytical cookies to improve your experience.

3. Use of Information

The information collected is used exclusively for sending newsletters, responding to inquiries, improving editorial content and displaying relevant contextual advertisements.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except when required by law.

5. Your Rights

You can request the deletion of your data at any time through our contact form.
